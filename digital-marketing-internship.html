What is Growth Marketing ?

Growth marketing is a strategy to increase a company's revenues by improving customer retention and loyalty.

How its different than Digital Marketing ?

Digital Marketing

Digital marketing is a broad term encompassing all marketing efforts using electronic devices or the internet. It includes:

  • Email Marketing
  • Content Marketing
  • SEO & SEM
  • Affiliate Marketing
  • Social Media Marketing
Online Marketing :

Online marketing is a subset of digital marketing focused specifically on internet-based channels, such as:

  • PPC Advertising
  • Display Ads
  • Social Media Advertising
  • Influencer Marketing
  • Webinars and Online Events

The Ultimate Guide to Digital Marketing Internship FAQs

  • What is Growth Marketing & How its different than Digital Marketing ?

    Growth Marketing focuses on experimenting and optimizing strategies to drive overall business growth. Digital Marketing involves using online channels like social media and search engines to promote products or services.

  • Who is this program for?

    The program is designed for:

    • Aspiring Digital Marketers: Individuals looking to start a career in digital marketing.
    • Marketing Professionals: Those wanting to update their skills or specialize further.
    • Business Owners: Entrepreneurs aiming to improve their online presence and drive growth.
    • Students: Learners seeking practical knowledge and experience in digital marketing.
    • Career Changers: Professionals from other fields who want to transition into digital marketing.

  • "Here’s Why You Need to Master Digital Marketing

    Mastering digital marketing is essential because it equips you with the skills to effectively reach and engage your audience online, drive targeted traffic, and boost your business’s growth in today’s digital world.

  • What is the duration of this program?

    [Part-time] If you can spend 15 hours per week, the program will take upto 9 months. You will start getting job offers from 6th month onwards

    [Full-time] If you can spend 30 hours per week, the program can be completed much faster and you can start getting offers post 4th month

  • Is this an online program?

    Yes. This is a online / offline program(Hybrid model).

  • How does the program work?

    Growapse is the world's most effective project-based learning platform for developers. Everything you learn in this program will revolve around building work-like professional projects in an actual developer environment. Learning through this program is quite literally similar to learning on-the-job or learning through actual work experience. While this may sound challenging, this is the best way to learn a new technology skill, and our mentors will be there to guide you through your journey. This is your chance to go beyond ineffective video based courses and bootcamps to upskill and revive your career with confidence.

  • Are the timings flexible during the program?

    You are expected to spend 15 hours a week to achieve the program goals. You can distribute the 15 hours across the week according to your work schedule without missing the program goals.

  • Can I get EMI if I don’t have a credit card?

    Yes, you can. No-cost EMI and low-cost EMI options are available through our partners

  • What if I decide to leave in the middle of the program?

    There will be no refund if you do not complete the program and leave in between.
